Your Mission

Should you choose to accept it...

You are a Treasure Hunter, using the newest in treasure hunting technologies: "Hot/Cold-ometer".

This technology works simply: Sweep your detector (cursor) back and forth over the field...Until you get a sense for your bearings! The readout will change from green to red if you get further from the treasure, and red to green as you get closer.

Happy hunting, best of luck, and enjoy your finds!
